About NoteQuest

NoteQuest is an interactive web application designed for high school and college students who want to better organize their notes, study more efficiently, and stay motivated throughout their academic journey. The platform transforms personal notes into dynamic study tools using artificial intelligence, allowing users to generate personalized quizzes, track academic performance, and apply effective study techniques within a gamified experience.
